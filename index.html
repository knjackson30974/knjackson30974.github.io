About Me

I've had an interest in all things web related since I was a kid growing up in the budding baby age of the internet. I was building crude html abomonations for fun, for school, you name it. I suppose we all start somewhere. I still remember trying to source the perfect side bared background for a school page about famous artists. Don't get me start on the wonderful page counters and cursor trails I integrated into everything.

I did not immediately picture my self working in tech (even though I am sure a lot of people did) and went to university to study anthropology and biology. Then it came down to whether I wanted to do more schooling and persue my real dream of a masters in anthropology that would maybe get me a cool museum job someday or rethink my path. So off to college I went where I earned my diploma as a computer programmer analyst.

I will admit I am not a die hard, like some people I have met along the way. I am trying to branch out and do some interesting projects on my own time amid renovating my house and hanging out with my dogs.
